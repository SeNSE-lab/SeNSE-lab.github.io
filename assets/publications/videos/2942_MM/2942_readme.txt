Video supplement for Identifying Times of Uncertainty in Whisker Sensing to Improve 3D Maps Generated by Whiskers, ICRA 2023 submission. The video includes a visual summary of the paper, with the important visual supplements such as recordings of experimental trials and sensor recordings (video's from the camera sensor).

Video is .mp4 file which was tested in the 2022 Media Player application in windows but should be playable by most software which plays .mp4.
