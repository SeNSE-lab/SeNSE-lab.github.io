Software and Datasets


Download WHISKiT Physics, a 3D dynamical model of the full rat vibrissal array which allows researchers to simulate the sensory input of the vibrissal system, i.e. the mechanical signals (moment and force) generated at the base of each whisker.

Download software (Elastica 2D, Elastica 3D) to compute the forces and moments at the base of a whisker based during quasistatic bending contact.

Download the geometry of the mouse whisker array and facial features. Data from Bresee et al., 2023.

Download the 2D geometry of over 500 rat whiskers Data from Belli et al., 2016.

Download an an analysis of the intrinsic curvature of the whiskers of ten different animal species Data from Luo and Hartmann, 2023.
